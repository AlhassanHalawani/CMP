# Canva AI Prompt for CMP Marketing Post

Paste the prompt below into Canva AI and adjust the CTA or wording if you want a different audience focus.

```text
Create a polished marketing post for "FCIT Clubs Management Platform (CMP)", the official platform for club life at the Faculty of Computing and Information Technology at King Abdulaziz University.

Goal:
Promote CMP as one unified platform that makes university club participation easier, more engaging, and more organized for students, club leaders, and staff.

Audience:
- Primary audience: students
- Secondary audience: club leaders and staff

Platform context:
CMP brings the full club experience into one place. Students can discover club activities, register for events, track attendance, and follow their progress. Club leaders and staff can manage clubs, oversee events, review attendance, monitor KPIs, and access reports. The platform replaces scattered manual processes with one clean digital experience.

Gamification context:
Show CMP as a motivating student experience, not just an admin tool. The platform uses XP, levels, badges, progress bars, streaks, and daily questions to make participation feel rewarding. Students earn XP through participation, unlock badges as milestones, build streaks through regular engagement, and see their progress visually. The feeling should be encouraging, achievement-driven, and community-focused.

Experience message:
The platform should feel smooth, modern, rewarding, and easy to use. Emphasize that CMP helps students join events faster, stay involved, track their growth, and feel recognized. For staff and club leaders, it improves organization, attendance verification, visibility, and reporting. Communicate that the experience is trusted, bilingual, and built for real campus workflows.

Visual design direction:
Match the existing CMP web app design as closely as possible.

- Use light-mode neobrutalism
- Pale lavender background
- White cards and panels
- Strong vivid purple accent color
- Black 2px borders
- Hard black offset shadows on cards and buttons
- Square corners, no rounded soft UI
- Subtle black grid background across the canvas, similar to a 56px grid
- Bold dashboard-inspired composition
- Heavy black headings with medium-weight supporting text
- Clean flat iconography for clubs, events, QR attendance, XP, badges, analytics, and reports
- Use UI-inspired shapes like statistic cards, event tiles, badge chips, progress bars, and a QR scan element
- Keep it high-contrast, playful, structured, and professional

Avoid:
- Dark mode
- Gradients
- Glassmorphism
- Soft shadows
- Blurry glow effects
- Glossy 3D objects
- Generic stock-photo SaaS style

Layout:
- Create a 1080 x 1350 portrait social media post
- Strong headline at the top
- Short supporting subtitle under it
- 4 to 6 feature tags or mini cards
- A central hero composition that feels like a product ad built from dashboard elements
- A clear CTA near the bottom

Suggested copy inside the design:
- Headline: Manage your FCIT club experience
- Subtitle: One platform for clubs, events, attendance, XP, badges, daily questions, KPIs, and reports
- Feature tags: Discover clubs / Register for events / QR attendance / Earn XP / Unlock badges / Track progress / Bilingual experience
- CTA: Join the CMP community

Tone:
Modern, student-centered, motivating, trustworthy, and energetic. The design should feel like a real extension of the CMP product, not a generic advertisement.

Important constraints:
- Do not invent fake statistics, ratings, testimonials, or user counts
- Do not add pricing, discounts, or sales language
- Do not use unrelated visuals
- Keep the post clean and readable
- Make the final design look like it belongs to the same product family as the CMP interface
```
